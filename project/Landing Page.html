FAQ

Questions, answered.

How does the AI know what questions to ask?
During setup you pick your trade — plumber, electrician, real estate, landscaper, and so on. We load a qualification playbook built specifically for that industry. You can tweak the questions any time.
What happens if the caller stops responding?
The AI waits, sends a single gentle nudge after about ten minutes, and if there's still no reply it closes the thread as "cold" and tags the lead accordingly. You never end up chasing no-shows.
Can I customise the qualification questions?
Yes. The default playbook covers most businesses, but every question is editable, and you can add your own. Pro and add-on plans unlock deeper branching logic.
How long does setup take?
About ten minutes. You tell us your industry, service area, and working hours; we generate your playbook; you forward missed calls with a single carrier setting. No new hardware.
Is there a contract or lock-in?
No. Month-to-month from day one. Cancel from the dashboard in two clicks. If we don't earn our keep in the first 30 days, we'll refund you.
What happens after the free trial?
On day 15 we email you. If you want to keep going, add a card and you're charged $197/month from that day. If you don't, the account pauses automatically — no charge, no chasing.
Do I keep my number if I cancel?
Yes. Your business number is always yours — we only handle forwarded missed calls. Remove the forwarding rule and you're back to square one, with zero data loss.
How does the agency partner program work?
Apply via the form in the Partner section. Once approved you get a referral link, a live agency dashboard, and 25% monthly recurring on every active client. Hit 20+ active clients and your rate steps up to 30% automatically.
