Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Who we are

Bury Digital ("we", "us", "our") operates an AI-powered missed call text back and lead qualification platform for real estate agents in Australia. This policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect personal information in accordance with the Australian Privacy Act 1988 and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs).

What we collect

  • From our clients (real estate agents): name, email address, phone number, business name, billing information, and account preferences.
  • From callers (your leads): phone number, SMS conversation content, and any information they voluntarily provide during the AI qualification conversation (such as property address, suburb, buying/selling intent, timeframe, and contact preferences).
  • From website visitors: standard analytics data including IP address, browser type, pages visited, and referring URLs.

How we collect it

Client information is collected when you sign up, subscribe, or contact us. Caller information is collected automatically when our system sends and receives SMS messages on your behalf via Twilio. Website data is collected via standard analytics tools.

Why we collect it

To provide our missed call text back and AI lead qualification service. To deliver qualified lead summaries to your hub. To send you SMS notifications when new leads come in. To process billing and manage your account. To improve our AI qualification accuracy and service quality. To communicate with you about your account, updates, and support.

Third-party services

We use the following third-party services to operate the platform:

  • Twilio — for sending and receiving SMS messages. Twilio processes caller phone numbers and message content. Twilio's privacy policy applies to their handling of this data.
  • Supabase — for secure database hosting and authentication. Your account data and lead data are stored in Supabase infrastructure.
  • Stripe (or your payment processor) — for processing subscription payments. We do not store your full credit card details.

We do not sell, rent, or trade any personal information to third parties for marketing purposes. Ever.

Data storage and security

All data is stored on secure, encrypted servers. SMS conversation data and lead summaries are stored in our Supabase database with row-level security policies. Access to personal data is restricted to authorised personnel only. We use HTTPS encryption for all data in transit.

Data retention

We retain client account data for as long as your account is active and for 30 days after cancellation. Lead data and SMS conversation history are retained for 12 months from the date of capture, after which they are automatically deleted unless you request earlier deletion. You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Withdraw consent for data processing.
  • Lodge a complaint with the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) if you believe we have breached the APPs.

Caller rights

Callers who interact with our AI SMS system can reply STOP at any time to opt out of further messages. Callers can contact us to request access to or deletion of their conversation data.

Cookies

Our website uses essential cookies for site functionality and analytics cookies to understand how visitors use our site. No advertising or tracking cookies are used.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the service constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
