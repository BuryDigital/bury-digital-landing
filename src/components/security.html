Security

Your data is safe with us.

We take security seriously because your leads and your clients' information deserve proper protection.

Encryption

All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted with TLS/SSL. Your lead data and SMS conversations are encrypted at rest in our database. API communications between our platform and third-party services (Twilio, Supabase) are encrypted end-to-end.

Infrastructure

Our platform is built on Supabase, which runs on AWS infrastructure with SOC 2 Type II compliance. Database access is protected by row-level security — each client can only access their own data. Automated backups run daily to prevent data loss.

Authentication

All accounts are protected by secure password hashing. Session tokens expire automatically after periods of inactivity. We recommend using a strong, unique password for your Bury Digital account.

Access control

Access to production systems is restricted to authorised Bury Digital personnel only. We follow the principle of least privilege — team members only have access to what they need. All access to client data is logged and auditable.

SMS security

SMS messages are sent through Twilio, a SOC 2 compliant communications platform trusted by hundreds of thousands of businesses globally. Phone numbers and message content are processed in accordance with Twilio's security standards and Australian telecommunications regulations.

Data minimisation

We only collect the data necessary to provide the Service. Callers can opt out of SMS conversations at any time by replying STOP. Lead data is automatically deleted after 12 months unless you request earlier deletion.

Incident response

In the unlikely event of a data breach, we will notify affected clients within 72 hours in accordance with the Notifiable Data Breaches scheme under the Privacy Act 1988. We will provide clear information about what happened, what data was affected, and what steps we are taking.

Questions?

If you have questions about our security practices, we're happy to walk you through them.
